STEP BY STEP INSTRUCTIONS ON HOW TO USE THE SYSTEM

How to add users to the system
  1. Visit the admin page at URL http://127.0.0.1:8000/admin/
  2. Go to AUTHENTICATION AND AUTHORIZATION section
  3. Go to Users section and click on the Add button
  4. Fill in the details of the new user and Save them
How to use the charts
  1. Click on the Charts folder icon in the Menu widget.
  2. Select the type of chart you want to view. For example choose Male vs. Female to view the chart representing the trend of HIV/AIDS infection between Males and Females.
How to the Geo-insights
  1. Click on the Geo-Insights folder icon in the Menu widget.
  2. Select the type of geo-insight you want. For example choose Patients to explore the demographical data of ART patients with respect to their district of residence.
How to Reports
  1. Click on the Reports folder icon in the Menu widget.
  2. Select the type of report you want. For example choose ART Challenges to view/download a report on the challenges patients face during ART.
